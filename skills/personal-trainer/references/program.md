## Weekly Progression

The program runs for at least 6 weeks. The key differences between weeks:

### Week 1 (Intro/Deload)
- All sets: RPE ~7-9 (no failure)
- Working sets: 2 per exercise
- No intensity techniques
- Purpose: learn the exercises, calibrate weights, ease in

### Weeks 2+
- Early Sets: RPE ~8-9
- Last Set: RPE 10 (failure)
- Working sets increase to 2-3 per exercise
- Intensity techniques applied on the last set as prescribed (Failure, Failure + LLPs, Myo-reps, Static Stretch)

### Progression Within Weeks
- Sets increase by ~1 per exercise from Week 1 to Week 2, then hold steady
- Reps and weight progress via double progression (reps first, then weight)
- Intensity techniques ramp up: Week 1 has none, Week 2+ adds Failure + LLPs, Myo-reps, and Static Stretches
