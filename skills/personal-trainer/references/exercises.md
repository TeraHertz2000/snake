options. Option 1 is not better than Option 2 - they are just different alternatives.

## Chest

### 45° Incline Barbell Press
- **Sub 1**: 45° Incline DB Press
- **Sub 2**: 45° Incline Machine Press
- **Cue**: 1 second pause at the bottom of each rep while maintaining tension on the pecs
- **Used in**: Upper (Strength) — 6-8 reps, 3-5 min rest

### Cable Crossover Ladder
- **Sub 1**: Pec Deck
- **Sub 2**: Bottom-Half DB Flye
- **Cue**: Do one set with low cable position, one set with medium-height, one with high cable position. If only 1-2 sets, choose your preferred 1-2 positions.
- **Used in**: Upper (Strength) — 8-10 reps, 1-2 min rest

### Barbell Bench Press
- **Sub 1**: Machine Chest Press
- **Sub 2**: DB Bench Press
- **Cue**: Set up a comfortable arch, quick pause on the chest and explode up on each rep
- **Used in**: Push (Hypertrophy) — 8-10 reps, 3-5 min rest

### Bottom-Half DB Flye
- **Sub 1**: Bottom-Half Seated Cable Flye
- **Sub 2**: Low-to-High Cable Crossover
- **Cue**: All reps performed in the bottom half of the ROM. Focus on feeling a deep stretch in your pecs at the bottom of each rep.
- **Used in**: Push (Hypertrophy) — 10-12 reps, 1-2 min rest

## Back

### Wide-Grip Pull-Up
- **Sub 1**: Wide-Grip Lat Pulldown
- **Sub 2**: Dual-Handle Lat Pulldown
- **Cue**: 1.5x shoulder width overhand grip. Slow 2-3 second negative. Feel your lats pulling apart on the way down.
- **Used in**: Upper (Strength) — 8-10 reps, 2-3 min rest

### Pendlay Deficit Row
- **Sub 1**: Smith Machine Row
- **Sub 2**: Single-Arm DB Row
- **Cue**: Stand on a bumper plate. Focus on getting a big stretch and touch your stomach/chest on each rep.
- **Used in**: Upper (Strength) — 6-8 reps, 2-3 min rest

### Neutral-Grip Lat Pulldown
- **Sub 1**: Neutral-Grip Pull-Up
- **Sub 2**: Dual-Handle Lat Pulldown
- **Cue**: Handle more out in front of you, like a cross between pullover and pulldown. Focus on feeling your lats working more than the weight you're using.
- **Used in**: Pull (Hypertrophy) — 8-10 reps, 2-3 min rest

### Chest-Supported Machine Row
- **Sub 1**: Chest-Supported T-Bar Row
- **Sub 2**: Incline Chest-Supported DB Row
- **Cue**: Flare elbows out at roughly 45° and squeeze your shoulder blades together hard at the top of each rep.
- **Used in**: Pull (Hypertrophy) — 8-10 reps, 2-3 min rest

### Neutral-Grip Seated Cable Row
- **Sub 1**: Helms Row
- **Sub 2**: Meadows Row
- **Cue**: Focus on squeezing your shoulder blades together, driving your elbows down and back.
- **Used in**: Pull (Hypertrophy) — 10-12 reps, 2-3 min rest

## Shoulders

### High-Cable Lateral Raise
- **Sub 1**: High-Cable Cuffed Lateral Raise
- **Sub 2**: Lean-In DB Lateral Raise
- **Cue**: Focus on squeezing your lateral delt to move the weight.
- **Used in**: Upper (Strength) + Push (Hypertrophy) — 8-12 reps, 1-2 min rest

### Machine Shoulder Press
- **Sub 1**: Cable Shoulder Press
- **Sub 2**: Seated DB Shoulder Press
- **Cue**: Ensure that your elbows break at least 90°. Mind-muscle connection with your delts. Smooth, controlled reps.
- **Used in**: Push (Hypertrophy) — 8-10 reps, 2-3 min rest

### 1-Arm 45° Cable Rear Delt Flye
- **Sub 1**: Rope Face Pull
- **Sub 2**: Reverse Pec Deck
- **Cue**: Pause for 1-2 seconds in the squeeze of each rep. Contract the rear delts hard.
- **Used in**: Pull (Hypertrophy) — 10-12 reps, 1-2 min rest

### Machine Shrug
- **Sub 1**: Cable Paused Shrug-In
- **Sub 2**: DB Shrug
- **Cue**: Brief pause at the top. Think about pulling your shoulders up to your ears.
- **Used in**: Pull (Hypertrophy) — 10-12 reps, 1-2 min rest

## Triceps

### Overhead Cable Triceps Extension (Bar)
- **Sub 1**: Overhead Cable Triceps Extension (Rope)
- **Sub 2**: DB Skull Crusher
- **Cue**: Optionally pause for 0.5-1 second in the stretched aspect of each rep.
- **Used in**: Upper (Strength) + Push (Hypertrophy) — 8-12 reps, 1-2 min rest

### Cable Triceps Kickback
- **Sub 1**: DB Triceps Kickback
- **Sub 2**: Bench Dip
- **Cue**: Choose upright or bent over - whichever feels more comfortable. In the full squeeze, your shoulder should be positioned back behind your torso.
- **Used in**: Push (Hypertrophy) — 12-15 reps, 1-2 min rest

## Biceps

### Bayesian Cable Curl
- **Sub 1**: Seated Super-Bayesian High Cable Curl
- **Sub 2**: Incline DB Stretch Curl
- **Cue**: If you have a left-right bicep size imbalance, do these 1 arm at a time starting with the weaker arm. Take the weaker arm to the listed RPE, then match reps with the other arm. If no imbalance, do both arms at the same time.
- **Used in**: Upper (Strength) — 8-10 reps, 1-2 min rest

### EZ-Bar Cable Curl
- **Sub 1**: EZ-Bar Curl
- **Sub 2**: DB Curl
- **Cue**: Set up the cable at the lowest position. Maintain constant tension on the biceps. Slow, controlled reps.
- **Used in**: Pull (Hypertrophy) — 10-12 reps, 1-2 min rest

### Machine Preacher Curl
- **Sub 1**: EZ-Bar Preacher Curl
- **Sub 2**: DB Preacher Curl
- **Cue**: Smooth, controlled reps. Mind-muscle connection with the biceps.
- **Used in**: Pull (Hypertrophy) — 12-15 reps, 1-2 min rest

## Quads

### Smith Machine Squat
- **Sub 1**: DB Bulgarian Split Squat
- **Sub 2**: High-Bar Back Squat
- **Cue**: Set up feet as normal squat, then bring them forward ~3-6 inches. This creates a more upright squat placing more tension on the quads. If heels raise, bring feet more forward. If feet slip or lower back rounds, bring feet back a bit.
- **Used in**: Lower (Strength) — 6-8 reps, 3-5 min rest

### Leg Press
- **Sub 1**: Smith Machine Static Lunge
- **Sub 2**: DB Walking Lunge
- **Cue**: Feet lower on the platform for more quad focus. Get as deep as you can without excessive back rounding. Control the negative and do a slight pause at the bottom of each rep.
- **Used in**: Legs (Hypertrophy) — 8-10 reps, 2-3 min rest

### DB Bulgarian Split Squat
- **Sub 1**: DB Step-Up
- **Sub 2**: Goblet Squat
- **Cue**: Lower all the way down until your front thigh is parallel to the ground. Drive through your front heel on the way up.
- **Used in**: Legs (Hypertrophy) — 8-10 reps, 2-3 min rest

### Leg Extension
- **Sub 1**: Reverse Nordic
- **Sub 2**: Sissy Squat
- **Cue**: Set the seat back as far as it will go while still comfortable. Grab the handles as hard as you can to pull your butt down into the seat. Use a 2-3 second negative. Feel your quads pulling apart on the negative.
- **Used in**: Lower (Strength) + Legs (Hypertrophy) — 8-12 reps, 1-2 min rest

## Hamstrings

### Lying Leg Curl
- **Sub 1**: Seated Leg Curl
- **Sub 2**: Nordic Ham Curl
- **Cue**: Set the machine so that you get the biggest stretch possible at the bottom. Prevent your butt from popping up as you curl.
- **Used in**: Lower (Strength) — 8-10 reps, 1-2 min rest

### Seated Leg Curl
- **Sub 1**: Lying Leg Curl
- **Sub 2**: Nordic Ham Curl
- **Cue**: Lean forward over the machine to get a maximum stretch in your hamstrings.
- **Used in**: Legs (Hypertrophy) — 10-12 reps, 1-2 min rest

### Barbell RDL
- **Sub 1**: DB RDL
- **Sub 2**: Snatch-Grip RDL
- **Cue**: To keep tension on the hamstrings, stop about 75% of the way to full lockout on each rep (stay in the bottom 3/4 of the range of motion).
- **Used in**: Lower (Strength) — 6-8 reps, 2-3 min rest

## Glutes and Hips

### Machine Hip Adduction
- **Sub 1**: Cable Hip Adduction
- **Sub 2**: Copenhagen Hip Adduction
- **Cue**: Mind-muscle connection with your inner thighs. These are great for adding thigh mass from the front. Push them hard.
- **Used in**: Legs (Hypertrophy) — 10-12 reps, 1-2 min rest

### Machine Hip Abduction
- **Sub 1**: Cable Hip Abduction
- **Sub 2**: Lateral Band Walk
- **Cue**: If possible, use pads to increase the range of motion on the machine. Lean forward and grab onto the machine rails to stretch the glutes further.
- **Used in**: Legs (Hypertrophy) — 10-12 reps, 1-2 min rest

## Calves

### Standing Calf Raise
- **Sub 1**: Seated Calf Raise
- **Sub 2**: Leg Press Calf Press
- **Cue**: 1-2 second pause at the bottom of each rep. Instead of just going up onto your toes, think about rolling your ankle back and forth on the balls of your feet.
- **Used in**: Lower (Strength) + Legs (Hypertrophy) — 6-12 reps, 1-2 min rest

## Abs

### Cable Crunch
- **Sub 1**: Decline Weighted Crunch
- **Sub 2**: Machine Crunch
- **Cue**: Round your lower back as you crunch. Maintain a mind-muscle connection with your 6-pack.
- **Used in**: Lower (Strength) — 8-10 reps, 1-2 min rest

### Roman Chair Leg Raise
- **Sub 1**: Hanging Leg Raise
- **Sub 2**: Modified Candlestick
- **Cue**: Allow your lower back to round as you curl your legs up. 10-20 reps is a broad range on purpose: just go until you hit the listed RPE with controlled form.
- **Used in**: Push (Hypertrophy) — 10-20 reps, 1-2 min rest
