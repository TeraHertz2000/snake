# Iran War Brief: 2025-2026

**Date:** March 1, 2026  
**Status:** Ongoing conflict - Day 2

---

## Executive Summary

A major war between Israel, the United States, and Iran began in June 2025 ("Twelve-Day War") and resumed in February 2026 following weeks of escalating tensions. The current phase began on February 28, 2026, with US-Israeli strikes that killed Supreme Leader Ali Khamenei and multiple senior Iranian commanders. Iran has launched retaliatory attacks across the region.

---

## Timeline of Conflict

### Pre-June 2025: Escalation
- **2024:** Israel and Iran exchanged strikes twice
- **May-June 2025:** Tensions built over Iran's nuclear program; diplomatic efforts failed

### June 2025: Twelve-Day War
- **Duration:** 12 days (June 12-24, 2025)
- Israel destroyed ~1/3 of Iran's ballistic missile launchers
- Israel destroyed ~35% of Iran's ballistic missile stockpile
- US strikes targeted Iranian nuclear facilities
- **Ceasefire:** June 24, 2025

### December 2025 - January 2026: Renewed Tensions
- Iran rebuilt missile stockpiles
- **January 5, 2026:** Israeli Security Cabinet authorized additional strikes on Iran

### February 28, 2026: Current War Begins
- **Operation Names:** "Roaring Lion" (Israel), "Operation Epic Fury" (US)
- US and Israel launched joint attack on Iran
- Targeted hundreds of military sites, key officials, military commanders, facilities
