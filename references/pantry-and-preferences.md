# Noah's Pantry & Dietary Profile

## Dietary Constraints
- **Vegetarian + canned tuna** (no other meat/fish)
- **Protein goal:** 100g per day
- **Calorie target:** 1,600–2,000 cal/day (usually closer to 1,600)
- **Breakfast:** Cold brew + 1 scoop protein powder. That's it. Don't plan breakfast meals.
- **Intermittent fasting practitioner** — respect the eating window.

## Preferred Protein Sources
- Select brand protein powder
- Greek yogurt
- Eggs
- Tofu (firm, extra-firm)
- Soy curls
- Impossible meat
- Canned tuna
- Canned beans (black, chickpeas, kidney, cannellini)
- Edamame (frozen)
- Lentils (dried)
- Cheese (feta, cheddar, parmesan, mozzarella)

## Recipe Sources (use these first)

- **It Doesn't Taste Like Chicken** — vegan classics (itdoesnttastelikechicken.com)
